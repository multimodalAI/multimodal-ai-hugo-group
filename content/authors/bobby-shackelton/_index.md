Bobby is a product and strategy leader with 25 years of experience turning complex data into meaningful, mission-driven platforms across climate, finance, and national security. From launching radar systems for the U.S. Air Force to building award-winning geospatial intelligence products at Bloomberg, He has consistently operated at the frontier of impact and innovation.

He has led transformative platforms at the intersection of AI, sustainability, and financial systems. As COO at RiskThinking.AI, He led the GTM of askRiskThinking.AI, the world’s first openly available AI platform for climate risk, and scaled adoption across 100+ institutions. As CPO at Climate Engine, he launched SpatiaFi, a Google Cloud award-winning platform used by Fidelity, BMO, Google, and the Bank of Canada, culminating in an acquisition by Earth Finance in 2025.

At Bloomberg, he founded the geospatial product division, delivering Terminal-integrated tools like MAP CLIMATE<GO> and MAP VIRUS<GO>. In 2017, he joined a United Nations working group to help define the TCFD Physical Climate Risk framework, introducing a spatial finance methodology that’s now embedded in global climate reporting standards.

At SNL Financial (now S&P Global), he launched SNL Maps from scratch, created and scaled the geospatial team globally, and later led the Excel Add-In and API platforms through a period of hypergrowth.

He is currently serve on the Advisory Board of the UK Open Multimodal AI Network (UKOMAIN), a £1.8M EPSRC-funded initiative advancing AI innovation across climate, space, and nature.

Throughout his career, He has shared insights at the White House, The Pentagon, World Bank, WWF, The Economist, Bloomberg Markets and global ESG forums. My work continues to be guided by one principle: building technologies that help people and the planet thrive, today and in the future.
